About

The Amur Leopard is one of the most endangered animals in the world, with only 35 living in the wilds of of the Russian Far East. This type of leopard can be distinguished from its relatives through its thick, pale coat, made for living in harsh, cold environments, particularly that of the Amur Basin (for which they are named).

Reason

I chose to research Amur Leopards because I like big cats (and I cannot lie), and because I think they need to be given more attention to be kept alive, since there are so few of them remaining.
